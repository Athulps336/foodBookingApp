ABOUT CAFE2020

Welcome to CAFE2020, where culinary excellence meets a warm and inviting atmosphere. Located in the heart of Kochi, our restaurant is a haven for food enthusiasts seeking a remarkable dining experience. We pride ourselves on delivering a menu that celebrates the art of gastronomy. Our talented team of chefs meticulously crafts each dish, combining the finest ingredients with innovative techniques to create culinary masterpieces that delight the senses. Indulge in a diverse range of flavors inspired by both local and international cuisines. From mouthwatering appetizers to sumptuous main courses and delectable desserts, our menu offers a harmonious blend of classic favorites and unique creations. We source our ingredients from trusted local suppliers, ensuring freshness and supporting our community.
